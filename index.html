Our website is a static one-page site and does not directly collect personal information from visitors such as names, email addresses, or contact details through forms or user accounts.

However, we do use a third-party service, Google Analytics, which collects non-personally identifiable information about your visit.

2. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics collects information such as:

  • Your IP address (anonymized)

This information is used in an aggregated and anonymous form to analyze website traffic and improve the website's content and performance. Google Analytics uses cookies (see section 3 below) to collect this information.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from sites that use their services by visiting: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

3. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. Google Analytics uses cookies to track your activity on our website.

Most web browsers are set to accept cookies by default. You can choose to set your browser to remove or reject browser cookies. Please be aware that if you choose to remove or reject cookies, this may impact your ability to use certain features of our website (although for a static one-page site, this impact is minimal).

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
